# WB Jewelry Series 8 Slides

## Purpose

This skill creates or improves a full 8-slide product card series for jewelry on Wildberries or similar marketplaces.

The series must:
- sell the product step by step;
- look visually unified;
- keep premium style;
- explain the product clearly;
- preserve exact item identity;
- improve conversion after click.

---

## General series rules

All slides must share:
- consistent typography;
- consistent color palette;
- consistent premium mood;
- consistent product appearance;
- consistent skin tone;
- consistent visual hierarchy.

Avoid:
- each slide looking like a different brand;
- random fonts;
- random text size;
- inconsistent product shape;
- overuse of icons;
- clutter.

---

## Recommended 8-slide structure

### Slide 1 — Main hero / CTR

Goal:
- stop attention;
- show product as expensive and desirable;
- make product understandable instantly.

Rules:
- minimal or no text;
- product large;
- face does not dominate;
- premium dark/neutral background;
- jewelry is hero.

---

### Slide 2 — Set composition

Goal:
- show what is included.

For 3-in-1 sets:
- necklace;
- earrings;
- bracelet/ring.

Rules:
- show all items clearly;
- avoid chaotic scattering;
- use clean arrangement;
- text can be: “КОМПЛЕКТ 3 В 1”.

---

### Slide 3 — Macro detail

Goal:
- prove quality through close-up.

Show:
- pearl texture;
- stone clarity;
- metal shine;
- chain detail;
- pendant detail if present.

Rules:
- sharp macro;
- premium light;
- no fake over-sparkle;
- detail must match original product.

---

### Slide 4 — Fit / size / length

Goal:
- answer scale and fit questions.

Show:
- how it sits on neck/wrist/ear;
- approximate length if known;
- visual scale on body.

Rules:
- do not invent exact dimensions if not provided;
- use visual comparison when numbers are unknown;
- keep product clear.

---

### Slide 5 — Occasion / styling

Goal:
- show where to wear it.

Suggested scenarios:
- evening;
- party;
- photoshoot;
- date;
- celebration;
- gift.

Rules:
- mood can be more lifestyle;
- product still must remain hero;
- avoid generic stock-photo vibe.

---

### Slide 6 — Gift / packaging

Goal:
- increase perceived value.

Show:
- pouch;
- card;
- box if available;
- gift presentation.

Rules:
- preserve real included items only;
- do not promise packaging that is not included;
- make it clean and premium.

---

### Slide 7 — Advantages / proof

Goal:
- summarize value.

Use only safe claims:
- “акцентный образ”;
- “для вечера и праздника”;
- “подходит для подарка”;
- “комплект украшений”;
- “выразительный блеск”.

Avoid unsupported claims:
- гипоаллергенно;
- не темнеет;
- натуральный жемчуг;
- серебро/золото;
- водостойкое;
unless proven.

---

### Slide 8 — Final emotional proof

Goal:
- leave premium impression;
- reinforce purchase desire.

Can show:
- full elegant look;
- product close-up on body;
- clean final composition;
- packaging + product.

Rules:
- not too much text;
- strong final brand impression;
- product remains clear.

---

## Typography rules

Use:
- one main font style;
- one secondary font style if needed;
- consistent sizes;
- large readable headings;
- minimal text.

Preferred:
- premium serif for headings;
- clean sans-serif for short explanatory text.

Avoid:
- tiny text;
- too many font weights;
- random capitalization;
- inconsistent text blocks.

---

## Infographic rules

Use only when it helps.

Good:
- 1–3 concise callouts;
- simple lines;
- clean labels;
- large icons only if readable.

Bad:
- many small icons;
- dense bullet lists;
- text over product;
- overdesigned marketplace clutter.

---

## Product consistency rules

Across all 8 slides:
- same product shape;
- same number of items;
- same stone/pearl structure;
- same material look;
- same necklace silhouette;
- same earring type;
- same bracelet/ring type.

If any slide changes the product:
- fail the series;
- revise that slide.

---

## Mandatory series autotests

Check:

1. Slide 1 has CTR power.
2. Slide 1 product is clear in thumbnail.
3. Slide 2 explains the set.
4. Slide 3 shows real detail.
5. Slide 4 answers scale/fit.
6. Slide 5 shows usage occasion.
7. Slide 6 shows gift/packaging only if real.
8. Slide 7 uses safe claims.
9. Slide 8 closes emotionally.
10. All slides share one visual language.
11. Fonts are consistent.
12. Product identity is consistent.
13. No slide has broken anatomy.
14. No slide has fake extra items.
15. No text overlaps key jewelry.
16. The full series feels like one premium card.

---

## Scoring

Score:
- Slide 1 CTR: /10
- Series consistency: /10
- Product preservation: /10
- Informational completeness: /10
- Premium feel: /10

If any score < 8.5:
- revise failed slide(s).

If final average < 9:
- continue correction up to 5 cycles.
